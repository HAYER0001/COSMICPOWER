## The Calorie Deficit Advantage

Weight loss ultimately comes down to calories in versus calories out. Makhana helps you maintain a deficit because it delivers high volume and crunch for relatively few calories. A generous handful (30g) is about 104 calories. Compare this to 30g of almonds (174 calories) or 30g of chips (162 calories).

## Protein Leverage

Protein is the most thermogenic macronutrient — your body burns about 20-30% of protein calories just digesting it. With 9.7g protein per 100g, makhana has a significant thermic effect. More importantly, protein triggers the release of appetite-suppressing hormones.

## Fibre Keeps You Regular

The fibre in makhana serves multiple purposes for weight loss:

1. **Bulk** — expands in your stomach, creating physical fullness
2. **Slow digestion** — prevents rapid glucose absorption
3. **Gut health** — feeds beneficial gut bacteria linked to healthy metabolism

## Blood Sugar Stability

The glycaemic index of makhana is estimated to be low (below 55), meaning it does not cause rapid blood sugar spikes. Spikes trigger insulin release, which tells your body to store fat. By keeping blood sugar stable, makhana helps your body remain in fat-burning mode.
