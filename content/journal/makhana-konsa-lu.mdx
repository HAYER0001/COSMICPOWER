> **Quick Answer:** Ready-to-eat snack chahiye? **Roasted makhana lo** — directly kha sakte ho, crunchy aur tasty. Cooking ya homemade masala banana hai? **Raw makhana lo** — ise roast karke apna flavour add kar sakte ho. Golden Deer Classic Roast already slow-roasted hai with a whisper of salt. Bas open karo aur khao.

## Yeh Sawal Kyo Aata Hai?

Jab bhi koi makhana kharidne jaata hai, do options hote hain: roasted aur raw. Dono ek hi seed se aate hain, lekin processing aur use alag hai. Yeh confusion bahut common hai.

## Roasted Makhana — Ready-to-Eat Crunch

Roasted makhana directly khaane ke liye ready hota hai. Isme flavour add kiya jaata hai — light salt, masala, cheese, tomato. Golden Deer ka Classic Roast cold-pressed oil mein slow-roasted hota hai, isme sirf teen ingredients hain: premium makhana, sunflower oil, aur fine sea salt.

**Kab lu mai roasted makhana?**

- Jab mujhe instant healthy snack chahiye
- Office ya travel mein le jaane ke liye
- Bina kisi preparation ke khaana ho
- Bachchon ke tiffin mein dene ke liye

## Raw Makhana — Cooking Ka Canvas

Raw makhana unroasted hota hai — ise aap ghar par roast kar sakte ho, kheer mein daal sakte ho, ya curry mein use kar sakte ho. Raw makhana ka apna koi strong taste nahi hota, isliye ye kisi bhi dish ke saath blend ho jaata hai.

**Kab lu mai raw makhana?**

- Ghar par apna masala banakar roast karna ho
- Kheer, curry, ya traditional recipe banani ho
- Bulk mein kharid kar business use karna ho
- White-label ya HoReCa supply ke liye

## Golden Deer Mein Kya Hai?

Golden Deer dono options provide karta hai. Retail D2C customers ke liye **5 roasted flavours** hain — Classic Roast, Pink Salt & Pepper, Tangy Tomato, Creamy Cheese, aur Spicy Masala. Bulk buyers ke liye **Premium Raw Makhana** available hai — 5–6 suta jumbo grade, hand-sorted, directly sourced from Bihar ke wetland farmers.

## Ekdum Simple Rule

- **Snack chahiye → Roasted Makhana**
- **Cook karna hai → Raw Makhana**
- **Dono try karna hai → Golden Deer ka range lo**
