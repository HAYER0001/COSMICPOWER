> **Quick Answer:** Makhana works in sweet and savoury dishes. Five-minute masala makhana, creamy makhana kheer, crunchy salad topper, makhana chaat, makhana curry, chivda-style trail mix, and chocolate-coated makhana for dessert. Use raw makhana for cooking and roasted for instant recipes. Golden Deer Classic Roast is the perfect base for any recipe.

## Recipe 1: 5-Minute Masala Makhana

**Best with:** Classic Roast or Raw Makhana

Heat 1 tbsp ghee in a pan. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp red chilli powder, and a pinch of amchur (dried mango powder). Toss in 2 cups of Golden Deer Classic Roast makhana. Stir for 2 minutes on low flame. Done. The ultimate 5pm snack.

## Recipe 2: Creamy Makhana Kheer

**Best with:** Raw Makhana

Soak 1 cup raw makhana in warm water for 15 minutes. Simmer 1 litre full-fat milk until it reduces by a third. Add the drained makhana, 1/2 cup sugar (or jaggery), crushed cardamom pods, and a pinch of saffron. Cook on low for 20 minutes until makhana is soft. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve warm or chilled.

## Recipe 3: Makhana Crunch Salad Topper

**Best with:** Classic Roast or Pink Salt & Pepper

Crush a handful of Golden Deer makhana into coarse bits. Sprinkle over any green salad in place of croutons or nuts. Works beautifully on a simple cucumber-tomato-onion salad with lemon juice and chaat masala.

## Recipe 4: Makhana Chaat

**Best with:** Classic Roast

Arrange 2 cups Classic Roast makhana on a plate. Top with finely chopped onion, tomato, cucumber, and boiled potato. Drizzle with green chutney, sweet tamarind chutney, and a dollop of yoghurt. Sprinkle chaat masala and fresh coriander. Instant chaat — no frying required.

## Recipe 5: Makhana Curry (Makhana Ki Sabzi)

**Best with:** Raw Makhana

Heat oil, add cumin, ginger-garlic paste, and finely chopped onion. Sauté until golden. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook until oil separates. Add 2 cups raw makhana and 1/2 cup water. Cover and simmer 10 minutes. Finish with cream and kasuri methi. Pairs beautifully with roti or naan.

## Recipe 6: Makhana Chivda (Trail Mix)

**Best with:** Classic Roast or Spicy Masala

Mix 2 cups Golden Deer makhana with 1/2 cup roasted peanuts, 1/4 cup roasted chana, 1/4 cup cashews, 2 tbsp raisins, 2 tbsp coconut flakes, and a pinch of black salt. Store in an airtight jar. The perfect travel snack that lasts for weeks.

## Recipe 7: Chocolate-Coated Makhana

**Best with:** Classic Roast

Melt 100g dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher) with 1 tsp coconut oil. Toss in 1.5 cups Classic Roast makhana until evenly coated. Spread on a parchment-lined tray and sprinkle with sea salt. Refrigerate 20 minutes. Healthy dessert makhana that tastes like a premium confection.

## Pro Tips

- Always use low flame when roasting makhana — high heat burns it quickly
- For recipes requiring soft makhana (kheer, curry), always use raw and soak first
- Golden Deer's Classic Roast is the most versatile base for quick recipes
- Store leftover makhana snacks in an airtight container — they stay crunchy for 5-7 days
